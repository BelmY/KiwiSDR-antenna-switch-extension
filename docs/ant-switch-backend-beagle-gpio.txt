This GPIO backend to KiwiSDR antenna switch extension

This backend controls Beaglebone green GPIO pins 70-76

http://wiki.seeed.cc/BeagleBone_Green/

Beaglebone green connector P8 pin 45 (GPIO 70) = Antenna 1
Beaglebone green Connector P8 pin 46 (GPIO 71) = Antenna 2
Beaglebone green Connector P8 pin 43 (GPIO 72) = Antenna 3
Beaglebone green Connector P8 pin 44 (GPIO 73) = Antenna 4
Beaglebone green Connector P8 pin 41 (GPIO 74) = Antenna 5
Beaglebone green Connector P8 pin 42 (GPIO 75) = Antenna 6
Beaglebone green Connector P8 pin 39 (GPIO 76) = Antenna 7

This GPIO backend to KiwiSDR antenna switch extension

http://wiki.seeed.cc/BeagleBone_Green/

This extension uses Beagle GPIO pins

Connector P8 pin 11 - GPIO 1_13 - Antenna 1
Connector P8 pin 12 - GPIO 1_12 - Antenna 2
Connector P8 pin 13 - GPIO 0_23 - Antenna 3
Connector P8 pin 14 - GPIO 0_26 - Antenna 4
Connector P8 pin 15 - GPIO 1_15 - Antenna 5
Connector P8 pin 16 - GPIO 1_14 - Antenna 6
Connector P8 pin 17 - GPIO 0_27 - Antenna 7
Connector P8 pin 18 - GPIO 2_1 - Antenna 8 (not available on webui yet)
Connector P8 pin 19 - GPIO 0_22 - Antenna 9 (not available on webui yet)
Connector P8 pin 26 - GPIO 1_29 - Antenna 10 (not available on webui yet)

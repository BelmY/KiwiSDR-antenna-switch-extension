This GPIO backend to KiwiSDR antenna switch extension

http://wiki.seeed.cc/BeagleBone_Green/

This extension uses Beagle GPIO pins

Connector P8
pin 11 = gpio_45 = Antenna 1
pin 12 = gpio_44 = Antenna 2
pin 14 = gpio_26 = Antenna 3
pin 15 = gpio_47 = Antenna 4
pin 16 = gpio_46 = Antenna 5
pin 17 = gpio_27 = Antenna 6 
pin 18 = gpio_65 = Antenna 7
pin 26 = gpio_61 = Antenna 8
